A plume of rich smoke escapes with her perfumed breath as she slurs that delicious, devastating, dreary, doting word. Daah-ling. Darling, would you be so kind? Darling, would you be so kind as to close the door when you leave? Such hypnotic words, such dark eyes. I left right that second and closed the door behind me with the gentlest touch.
