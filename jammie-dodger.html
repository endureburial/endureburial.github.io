Jammie Dodgers

How unbearably adorable! Mon coeur, mon chou… my love and devotion eternal! I love you. I love you and so, if you let me, I will for one delicious moment dissolve into a small puddle. I shall deliquesce in this very spot so that you might scoop me up from the ground and carry me with you in a flask on your belt.

I shall swallow 4 cups of sugar and boil until I become strawberry jam. I shall pour myself into your core; I am a little heart shape on your torso. I am a passenger that sits with you always. I am content. Non dire una parole che non sia d’amore. I am yours.
