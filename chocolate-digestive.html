Chocolate Digestive

You saucy minx! You utter devastation! Don’t think I don’t notice your coy smile, your exquisite eyelashes. Don’t think I don’t notice you looking for small openings in my body so that you may carefully seep inside of me and arrive at the centre to finally break my pulsating heart. I see it in your lips; I knew it in your eyes.

And, after all of that, I would let you do it again. I would take this heartbreak intravenously; I would take this infinite pleasure in the most painful way possible if it meant those cruel eyes and sly mouth were the last things I saw.
