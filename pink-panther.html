Pink Panther Wafer

Pure Uncut Hedonism. Pink Panther Wafers gave me ego death for 7 sweet seconds. They opened the doors of perception to reveal nothing but a small man wearing Vans and a suit in the corner of the abyss who proceeded to pitch his idea for a tech start-up to me. Pink Panther Wafers are cartoon biscuits. They tread the 4th dimension with mesmerising grace and call to you in the dead of night. They are not your friends. Pink Panthers will betray you for a packet of Walkers Baked crisps. They will grow damp in your mouth but send your senses into overdrive. Pink Panthers remind you that you are mortal.
