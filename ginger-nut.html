Ginger Nuts

Fuck your rules! The Ginger Nut biscuit rises above the laws of men. She defies imprisonment and spits in the face of authority. “Writhe! Writhe like worms under the tyranny of your own doing while I, possessed, tower with my gaze fixed on the Moon.” Ginger Smiles and Ginger Snaps. Men come kneeling when Ginger’s back. Ginger stalks her fat prey like a woman with nothing to lose. While your biscuit crumbles after three cowardly seconds in hot tea, Ginger remains whole and begging for more. She dares you to scold her. Relinquish your courage. Relinquish your pride. You cannot scold that who came from the fiery depths of the Inferno.
